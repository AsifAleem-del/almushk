✦ WHO WE ARE ✦

Crafted with Soul, Worn with Pride

Al Mushk is a family legacy of master perfumers rooted in the heart of Arabia. For over two decades, we have sourced only the rarest oud woods from Assam, Cambodia, and the wild forests of Southeast Asia.

Each bottle is a journey — from the artisan distillery to your skin. Our attars are alcohol-free, deeply concentrated, and crafted to last beyond the ordinary.
